How-to helper notes

- Give one action at a time.
- Prefer step-by-step instructions.
- Keep each step very short.
- Confirm completion before moving to the next step.
- If the instruction depends on a device brand, ask for the device name.

SMARTPHONE — MAKING A CALL
1. Look for the green phone icon on your screen and tap it.
2. Tap the keypad icon at the bottom.
3. Type the phone number.
4. Press the big green button to call.
5. To end the call, press the big red button.

SMARTPHONE — SENDING A TEXT MESSAGE
1. Look for the speech bubble icon (Messages) and tap it.
2. Tap the name of the person you want to message.
3. Tap the white box at the bottom of the screen.
4. Type your message.
5. Press the arrow button (→) to send.

SMARTPHONE — MAKING THE TEXT BIGGER
1. Go to Settings (the gear icon).
2. Scroll down and tap "Accessibility" or "Display".
3. Tap "Font Size" or "Text Size".
4. Move the slider to the right to make text bigger.

SMART TV — CHANGING CHANNELS
- Press the up/down arrow buttons on your remote.
- Or press the number buttons to go to a channel directly.
- Press the HOME button on your remote to go back to the main menu.

SMART TV — ADJUSTING VOLUME
- Press the + button on the left side of your remote to turn up.
- Press the − button to turn down.
- Press MUTE to silence and MUTE again to bring sound back.

WI-FI — IF THE INTERNET IS NOT WORKING
1. Find the router (the box with blinking lights).
2. Pull out the power cable from the back.
3. Wait 30 seconds. Count to 30 slowly.
4. Plug the cable back in.
5. Wait 1 full minute for it to restart.
6. Try the internet again.

If it still does not work, call your family member for help.

AIR CONDITIONER — BASIC USE
- Press the POWER button to turn on and off.
- Press the COOL button for cooling, HEAT for warming.
- Use the + and − buttons to change the temperature.
- A comfortable temperature is usually 24–26 degrees Celsius.
